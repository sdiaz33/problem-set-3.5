All About Bears

This is an introductory page to the world about bears. This website will discuss bears, different species, their environments, diets and current news about them. It is known that there are several species of bears, but this website only focuses on three to define them as accurately and specifically as possible.

  • Brown bears
  • Grizzly bears
  • Polar bears
